About US

FullyBeyond is the CSUF Designathon 2024 theme, inviting you to leapfrog conventional design thinking and explore the unexplored. Here, we'll delve deep into the vibrant world of inclusivity and diversity as the cornerstones of our design process.

Just like a lush rainforest teems with life, design flourishes when it embraces a variety of perspectives. We believe in empathy, equity, and celebrating differences. By going Fully Beyond, we can unlock the power of design to shape a future that reflects the richness of our ever-evolving society.

FAQ

What is a designathon?

A designathon is an intensive event where participants work solo or in teams to design, develop, and present innovative solutions to specific challenges within a set timeframe.

How do I participate?

Visit our registration google form at [link] and follow the instructions on the form.

How do I join the FullyBeyond Discord?

Once you have been accepted into FullyBeyond after registering yourself as a participant, you will be sent an email with the Discord link.

Do I need a team to participate?

Your team can have a minimum of 1 member (yes, you can participate solo!) to a maximum of 4 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! You can find others like you looking for a team to work with on our discord!

What resources are available for participants?

We created a Participant Guide handbook [link] for those who are completely new to Designathons and need a push in the right direction.

Where is the event?

Fullybeyond is a virtual event held on Discord!

What are the prompts?

Details of the prompts will be released on Discord at 11 AM on November 9th.

How are submissions evaluated?

Your submissions are evaluated based on how well you visually present and verbally explain the concept of your solution, user research, and your design process.
